# Propose Special-Terms Clauses

**Plugin skill:** `oxels-propose-special-terms-clauses`

Standardize how special terms are papered. Read the special-terms language on the order forms of recently closed-won deals (default the last two weeks; the window is adjustable), cluster the clauses by what they do rather than how they are worded, and propose one canonical wording per recurring special term — so the same term is always written the same way. Each candidate is surfaced with its source order forms and quoted evidence, and genuine differences between variants are flagged rather than papered over. Proposal-only and read-only.
